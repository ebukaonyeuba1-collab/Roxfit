Hi, I'm Roxanne Obi 👋

Lagos-born, Lagos-raised, and obsessed with helping women look and feel their most powerful selves. I'm a certified personal trainer, nutritionist, and the proudest big sister you'll ever meet.

I started RoxFit after years of following Western programs that didn't account for our bodies, our food, our climate, or our daily reality. Every program I tried was designed for someone else. I knew there had to be a better way.

Today, RoxFit has helped over 50,000 women across Nigeria and West Africa transform not just their physiques — but their confidence, their energy, and their entire relationship with their bodies.
